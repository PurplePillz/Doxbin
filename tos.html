Here at Doxbin, we supply users with the ability to upload text information without the fear of censorship. Most pastes won't come down without a court order. What this means is that if your info goes up, it's not coming down unless it's inaccurate, breaks our TOS or we recieve a court order from our server hosted country.

What is Doxbin?

Doxbin is a document sharing and publishing website for text-based information. There are no limitations on what info you can post as long as it's not spam, child explicit material or violating hosting country jurisdictional laws. For example, kiddie porn links, your mothers creditcard and any support of terrorism/physical violence is not allowed here.

Doxbin is not your personal army, nor your personal slam page.

User information:

The only information we keep is the information you give us upon registration (email address & hashed password).

IP addresses are not retained in the access logs (In fact, even if someone tcpdumped everything, it would just show a bunch of traffic coming from 127.0.0.1)
Access logs are kept for up to 48 hours.

The only "real" information we obtain is your user agent string which is wiped from our servers after 7 days.

Rules & takedown requests:

Pastes that break our TOS are subject to be removed.
Here are our list of rules that you must comply with. if you don't, they will be removed.

Content that is not allowed on Doxbin:
- Third party links to underage explicit images
- Pastes that don't meet our information minimum requirements
- Any details/information specifically about children under the age of 15
- Dox requests
- Spam
- IPloggers/infected files
- Reposting the same dox

FAQ:

Can users remove their own pastes?
Upgraded users can private/unlist their own pastes. We don't allow non-ranked users to remove their pastes. (Ranks purchasable at https://doxbin.com/upgrade)
Can I edit my paste?
Yes, as long as you posted it on your account. If you pasted something anonymously, you will not be able to edit the paste (no exceptions)
Why was my paste removed?
Pastes can be removed for a number of reasons. Please read above for the list of content that's not allowed on our service.
How long will valid pastes stay up?
Forever (as long as the service stays up, or the pastes db will be handed to the next owner)
Can I pay for a blacklist?
Yes, message @Brenton or @Doxer on Telegram for blacklist requests
